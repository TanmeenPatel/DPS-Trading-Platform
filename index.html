DASHBOARD

DISCLAIMER:
This trading platform is an extremely gameified version of the stock market and does not represent the stock market in any way. The stock prices and the news associated are all fake.

INSTRUCTIONS:
You start with 100,000. You can use this money to buy shares in 5 companies. Every now and then, the game will send you an alert with a breaking news about any one of the companies. You have some time to decide whether you wish to sell or buy shares before the game announces if the stock value increases or decreases. Beware, the game randomly chooses if the value will increase or decrease.

TIMINGS:
You have a total time of 10 minutes. The first news will come in a minute.
